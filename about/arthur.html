Arthur Hatchuel

French

Voice Part: Tenor
Year: Graduate
Member From: Fall 2015
Major: Civil and Environmental Engineering
Hometown: Paris, France
Arrangements: None

Music Interests: It's a secret ;)

Bio: Arthur is the only graduate student of DtS. Probably because he's French and "understand not very good English", he doesn't realize that it's a bit offbeat for a grad student to hang out with a group of Glee-fan undergrads, and he even seems to appreciate it. Spontaneous and usually quiet, Arthur sometimes has to moderate the vibrato he acquired during his five years of classical chorale in France. Arthur likes playing the piano for hours, reading intellectual novels that he doesn't really understand, going to contemporary art fairs and travelling to very far away countries to take thousands of photos.
DISCLAIMER: NOT ALL DTS MEMBERS ARE GLEE FANS!

Random: I taught the rest of DtS a very challenging vocal warmup and it was satisfying to hear them struggle.
