ABOUT US

Drawn to Scale (DtS) is a co-ed a cappella group at UC Berkeley composed of a diverse group of dedicated, caring, and talented students. They continuously strive to maintain a high standard of musicality while bringing student-made arrangements to life in exciting and innovative ways. From pop to contemporary, rock, indie, R&B, DtS has a wide-ranging repertoire ready to perform for any event. If you are interested in booking DtS, find our contact at our bookings page.
